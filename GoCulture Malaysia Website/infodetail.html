Traditional Music and Dance

Discover the soul of Malaysia through the harmonious fusion of music and dance

The Joget is a traditional Malay dance often performed during celebrations and weddings. It features couples dancing gracefully in pairs to rhythmic music.

Joget

Malay

The Lion Dance is a traditional Chinese dance performed during festivals and celebrations. Dancers mimic the movements of a lion, often accompanied by martial arts displays.

Lion Dance

Chinese

It's often performed during the Harvest Festival (Pesta Kaamatan) and features rhythmic footwork and graceful arm movements.

Sumazau

Kadazan-Dusun

The gendang is a traditional Malay drum used in various cultural and religious ceremonies. It comes in different sizes and is often played in ensembles.

Malay Gendang

Malay

The tabla is a pair of hand-played drums, consisting of two different-sized drums, the "dayan" (treble) and the "bayan" (bass). It's commonly used in classical Indian music.

Tabla

Indian

Guzheng is a traditional Chinese zither with numerous strings and movable bridges. It produces a melodic and soothing sound and is often featured in Chinese orchestras.

Gu Zheng

Chinese

Traditional Foods

Local Traditional Foods

Satay consists of skewered and grilled meat (often chicken, beef, or lamb), served with a flavorful peanut sauce and pressed rice (nasi impit).

Satay

Malay

Roti canai is a type of flatbread that's crispy on the outside and soft on the inside. It's commonly served with dhal (lentil curry) or various other curries

Roti Canai

Indian

Char kway teow is a stir-fried noodle dish featuring flat rice noodles, prawns, cockles, Chinese sausage, and bean sprouts, cooked in a savory soy sauce.

Char Kway Teow

chinese

Kuih are various types of colorful and intricately designed Nyonya desserts, often made with ingredients like coconut, rice flour, and palm sugar.

Nyonya Kuih

nyonya

Hinava is a traditional Kadazan-Dusun dish made with fresh raw fish marinated in lime juice, chili, and shallots. It's similar to the Iban umai.

Hinava

Kadazan

Manuk pansuh is a dish prepared by cooking chicken meat in a bamboo stalk, filled with water, seasonings and covered with tapioca leaves from the cassava plant

Manok Pansoh

Iban

Baju Kurung

Indian women often wear the "sari," a long piece of fabric draped elegantly around the body, accompanied by a blouse.

Sari

Indian

The "cheongsam" for women or "qipao" is a form-fitting, high-necked dress with a straight cut. It's commonly associated with Chinese New Year celebrations or weddings.
